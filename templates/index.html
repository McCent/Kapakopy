Who We Are & What Do We Do

KAPA Management Services was established in 2002 by Paul Needham. We are building contractors based in the south dublin area. We specialise in providing complete and comprehensive services to residential home owners, architects and engineers.

Attention to detail is the difference between a good home and a great home. It's all the small details and decisions made at design and build phase that come together to deliver a really high quality finish.
